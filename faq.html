Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about Sijing × World Famous Cities and our cultural comparison journeys

What is "Sijing × World Famous Cities" about?

Sijing refers to China's Four Great Ancient Capitals (Beijing, Nanjing, Xi'an, Luoyang). This project pairs each of them with a famous world city to explore cultural similarities, differences, historical connections, and modern exchanges through stories, images, and comparisons.

Why pair ancient Chinese capitals with Western/Eastern cities?

To highlight shared human experiences across civilizations — such as imperial power, architectural grandeur, religious heritage, and cultural innovation — while celebrating the unique beauty of each city.

Are the pairings based on historical facts?

Some pairings have direct historical links (e.g., Silk Road between Xi'an and Rome), while others are thematic and comparative (e.g., imperial elegance in Beijing and romance in Paris). All content aims to inspire curiosity and appreciation rather than claim exact equivalence.

Can I suggest new city pairings?

Yes! We welcome suggestions. Please contact us via the Contact Us page with your ideas.

Is there a mobile app or printable version?

Not yet, but the site is fully mobile-responsive. You can save pages as PDF using your browser's print function for offline reading.

How can I contribute photos or stories?

We'd love to feature user-submitted content! Send your photos, travel stories, or cultural insights to info@sijingcities.com. Selected contributions may be featured with credit.

Is the site free to use?

Yes, completely free for personal and educational use. All content is created for cultural appreciation and is non-commercial.
